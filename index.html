Multi OBS Controller

Control multiple OBS Studio instances in an easy synchronous way via Stream Deck

Keep your OBS Studio instances synced or manage them individually with a single Stream Deck action
Compatible with OBS Studio 29.0+. Get from Elgato Marketplace and follow the simple configuration steps
Keys responsive to events. Always know the current OBS Studio states associated with your action
